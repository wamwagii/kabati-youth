About Kabati Youth FC

Founded in 2016, The Bees have risen from grassroots football to the National Super League, nurturing local talent and building community through the beautiful game.

Our Story

Kabati Youth FC was established in 2016 with a vision to provide a platform for young football talents in Murang'a County. Starting as a community initiative, the club has grown into a professional football organization competing in the National Super League.

Our journey from grassroots football to the national stage is a testament to the dedication of our players, coaches, and the unwavering support of our community.

Our Vision
