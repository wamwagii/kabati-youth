Our History

From Humble Beginnings to National Stage

Kabati Youth FC was established in 2016 with a vision to nurture local talent and provide a platform for young footballers in Murang'a County to showcase their skills.

Starting from grassroots football, the club quickly rose through the ranks, earning promotion to the National Super League through determination, skill, and community support.

Today, we continue to build on our legacy, developing not just footballers but responsible citizens who contribute positively to society.

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To become a leading football academy and professional club in Kenya, recognized for developing world-class talent and promoting sports excellence within our community.

Our Mission

To identify, nurture, and develop young football talent through professional coaching, education, and character building, creating opportunities for success both on and off the pitch.

Our Core Values

Community

We are rooted in our community and committed to its growth and development.

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in everything we do, both on and off the pitch.

Integrity

We conduct ourselves with honesty, fairness, and respect for all.

Development

We are committed to the holistic growth of our players as athletes and individuals.

Community Engagement

Giving Back

Kabati Youth FC is more than just a football club; we're an integral part of the Murang'a community. Our players and staff regularly participate in community initiatives, including:

  • Football clinics for local schools
  • Environmental clean-up campaigns
  • Charity matches for local causes
  • Mentorship programs for youth
